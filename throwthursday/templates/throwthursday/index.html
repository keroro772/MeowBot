Here you can sign up to a 1v1 Throwdown Thursday with Meow in Rocket League! Every Thursday Meow will battle the viewers in the list who have not already 1v1'd him in descending order.

If a challenger is successful in defeating Meow they shall be awarded 3 points.
If they lose, however, they shall receive only 1 point.
And if they do not show up, they receive nothing.
Once the month is over the challengers shall receive ranks based on how many points they managed to gain.

A future event is being considered in which the challengers in each rank brawl eachother and the winner in each rank shall receive a prize depending on the rank they are in.

Anyone is welcome to take part in the 1v1 Throwdown, no matter their platform of choice, whether it is PC, Playstation, or Xbox.
