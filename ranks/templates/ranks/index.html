Catnip Ranks

Ranks are based on your view time, you get 1 catnip every 1 mins.

The percentage is calculated based on your position to everyone else who has catnip.

Use !myrank to find out yours and !catnip to find out your exact amount of catnip.

Use !watchtime to find out how long you have watched in days and hours.
